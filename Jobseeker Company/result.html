    FPT

    FPT Software is a global software outsourcing and technology company based in Vietnam. It is part of FPT Corporation, a multinational information technology company of Vietnam

    Address: 32 Hoang Mai - Dong Da - Ha Noi


    REQUIREMENTS

    - Bachelor or Engineer’s degree in Computer Sciences or related fields;

    - At least 3-5 years of experience in tester role in web and/or mobile application;

    -Fluent in English. Speaking French is an advantage;

    -Good communication skill with ability to collaborate with both on-site and overseas colleagues;

    -Willing to travel.

    JOB DESCRIPTIONS

    - Understand new feature/functionality defined in product requirement by product owners and define the testing scope required through release testing process.

    -Write test case, test plan and execute test the mobile/web applications

    - Create unit test cases, integration test case, execute test case.

    - Perform regression test, performance test, load test, stress test.

    -Report and manage bugs found while testing.

    BENEFITS

    - Attractive salary with 13th-month pay, competitive reward and benefits according to work performance & achievements;

    - Social insurance, health insurance and unemployment insurance according to Vietnamese Labor Law;

    - Complementary private health insurance;

    - Continuous training and development on the company’s products;

    - Professional, innovative and dynamic work environment;

    - Exciting company activities.
